Notable Figures

Meet the legendary warriors, scholars, and leaders of Ohafia

Our Heroes

Legends of Ohafia

Chief Kalu Ezera

Scholar & Statesman

A distinguished academic and political figure who served Nigeria with distinction, bringing honor to Ohafia on the national stage.

Chief Nna Okereke

Legendary Warrior

One of the most celebrated Ohafia warriors, known for his bravery in battle and his dedication to protecting his people.

Queen Ugwueke

Traditional Ruler

A revered queen mother who played a crucial role in preserving Ohafia's cultural traditions and guiding her people with wisdom.

Dr. Chukwuma Akuma

Medical Pioneer

A pioneering physician who established healthcare facilities in Ohafia and trained generations of medical professionals.

Chief Ibe Nwosu

Master Builder

The visionary architect behind many of Ohafia's grand mansions, blending European elegance with African tradition.

Chief Okoronkwo Oji

Cultural Custodian

Dedicated his life to preserving and promoting Ohafia's cultural heritage, including the famous war dance traditions.
